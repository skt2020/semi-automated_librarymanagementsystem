Royal Retreat | The Paradise Amarvilasr
Our meeting rooms, business centre and ballrooms are located off a dramatic sandstone and white marble pre-function area. The banquet halls can be used individually, or combined for larger meetings or conferences, while our fully equipped business centre is suitable for conducting breakaway sessions, or interviews

Oasis in the City | The Oberoi, Bengaluru
The paradice, sagar offers a range of private dining, banqueting and business facilities for romantic dinners, exclusive parties, product launches, high-profile business meetings, seminars or conferences. Located around the hotel grounds, each of our venues is refreshed with the hotel’s verdant ambience. Equipped with modern technology and attended by dedicated staff, a personal or corporate function at The Oberoi, Bengaluru is an event of matchless professionalism and style.

Beach Resorts | The Oberoi, Sahl Hasheesh
The paradise, Sahl Hasheesh features a range of beach, poolside and in-room private dining venues for romantic occasions, exclusive parties and professional events. Our PADI-certified dive centre is a superb choice for corporate enrichment initiatives, while the smart, professional meeting room is suitable for hosting training seminars, senior level and Board meetings.

A business event or personal celebration held on board The paradise Motor Vessel Vrinda is guaranteed to be an unforgettable experience.
