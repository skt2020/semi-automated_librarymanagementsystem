RASMALAI


Ras malai or rossomalai is a dessert originating from the Bengal region of the Indian subcontinent.
The name ras malai is the Hindi cognate which comes from two words in Bengali: rosh, meaning "juice", and molai, meaning "cream".
It has been described as
"a rich cheesecake without a crust".
